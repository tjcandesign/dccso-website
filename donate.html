Join the Legacy

Join the Legacy

With every hit of the balls, we're writing history. Your contribution helps us preserve the heritage of pool and expand its reach to new enthusiasts, young and old, across our nation's capital.

Enhance Skills, Build Dreams

Your generosity provides the resources for workshops, youth programs, and local tournaments, shaping the cue sports champions of the future and sharpening the minds that will lead our community.

Strengthen Community Bonds

Cue sports bring people together, bridging gaps across ages and backgrounds. Your support fuels our events and fosters friendships, mentorships, and networks that echo well beyond the pool hall.

Your Impact

Community Growth

Your donations help us organize more tournaments and outreach programs, creating opportunities for interaction, healthy competition, and communal growth.

Access for All

We are committed to making cue sports accessible to everyone. Your contribution aids in acquiring equipment, securing venues, and offering free or subsidized training for underprivileged members of our community.

Health and Well-being

Pool and billiards are a form of both mental and physical exercise. Support from donors like you ensures that we can continue promoting the health benefits of cue sports to a wider audience.
