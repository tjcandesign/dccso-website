DC COMMUNITY CUE SPORTS ORGANIZATION NON-PROFIT

Welcome to the District Community Cue Sports Organization

Welcome to the beating heart of pool and cue sports in Washington, D.C. Our non-profit organization is dedicated to promoting sportsmanship, skill development, and community engagement through the timeless game of pool. We invite you to join us in this endeavor that goes beyond the felt and into the fabric of our society.

Our Mission

The District Community Cue Sports Organization is more than just a name; it's a Commitment.

We strive to create a network of pool enthusiasts and professionals, fostering an environment where the clack of pool balls is the sound of community coming together.

Our Vision

We envision a thriving community where the strategic play of cue sports acts as a catalyst for connection, development, and engagement across all walks of life in Washington, D.C. Our aim is to be the cornerstone of a vibrant network where the values of sportsmanship, inclusion, and the pursuit of excellence in pool are celebrated and upheld.

Through fostering this dynamic and inclusive environment, we strive to enhance the cultural fabric of our community, ensuring cue sports are accessible to all and remain a cherished legacy for generations to come.

Special Events Highlight

  • Meet and Greets: Rub shoulders with local celebrities and cue sports legends.
