Promoting Excellence in Cue Sports throughout the D.C. Pool Community & beyond.

We are the beating heart of pool and cue sports in Washington, D.C. Our non-profit organization is dedicated to promoting sportsmanship, skill development, and community engagement through the timeless game of pool.

We invite you to join us in this endeavor that goes beyond the felt and into the fabric of our society.

Welcome to the District Community Cue Sports Organization Non-profit!

About Our Organization

Show your support through financial donations or by joining one of our committees.

KTG - Know The Game

KTG: Know The Game is an immersive beginner's program designed to introduce participants to the fundamentals of pool, including cue techniques and game rules. It offers personalized instruction and practice play, fostering a welcoming community for all new enthusiasts of pool.
