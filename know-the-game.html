KTG: Know The Game – Your Gateway to the World of Pool

Step into the realm of cue sports with our "Know The Game" program, a comprehensive and engaging pool lesson event tailored for beginners.

Whether you've never held a cue or you're looking to refine your basic skills, KTG is your perfect starting point.

Re-registration for Lessons Opens Soon!

Program Overview

KTG: Know The Game is more than just a series of lessons; it's an invitation to join a community that shares your newfound passion for pool. Our expert instructors will guide you through the fundamentals of the sport in a supportive and interactive environment.

By the end of the program, you won't just understand the game; you'll embody the poise and confidence of a seasoned player starting their journey.

What You'll Learn

Cue Mechanics

Master the proper grip, stance, and stroke that lay the foundation for all cue sports.

Game Rules

Dive into the diverse world of pool games, learning the rules and strategies of 8-ball, 9-ball, and more.

Shot Techniques

Discover how to aim, execute and add spin to your shots, giving you control and precision on the table.

Etiquette and Sportsmanship

Embrace the code of conduct that makes pool a gentleman's game and a sport of noble character.

Program Features

  • Hands-On Instruction: Receive individual attention in a group setting to ensure you're hitting the right notes.
  • Practice Play: Put theory into practice with supervised match play, reinforcing your skills in real-game scenarios.
  • Resource Materials: Get access to learning aids that you can take home to study and improve between sessions.
  • Community Access: Engage with fellow enthusiasts and seasoned players in our community forums and events.

Women's Community Coaching

DC Women's Community Coaching aims to provide a supportive environment for women of all skill levels to improve their pool skills. Led by experienced coaches It's a perfect opportunity to learn, make new friends, and become part of a community passionate about pool.
